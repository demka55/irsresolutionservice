How we help

From IRS trouble to fully resolved

Whether you're years behind on filing, facing an audit, or dealing with a collections notice — Romeo has helped hundreds of people in exactly your situation.

01

Haven't filed in years?

The IRS requires you to file, even if you can't pay. The good news: filing late returns — even years late — stops penalties from growing and opens the door to resolution options like installment plans and offers in compromise. Romeo handles this for individuals and businesses going back as many years as needed.

02

Received an IRS notice or audit letter?

Most people panic when they get a letter from the IRS. Romeo spent years on the other side writing those letters — he knows exactly what each one means, what the IRS is actually asking for, and the best strategy to respond. Use our free notice decoder below, or contact us for full representation.

03

Owe back taxes you can't pay?

The IRS has programs most people don't know about — installment agreements, Currently Not Collectible status, Offers in Compromise, and penalty abatement. Romeo negotiates directly with the IRS to find the option that minimizes what you owe and protects your assets.

04

Facing an audit or appeal?

Romeo audited businesses as an IRS Revenue Agent — he knows the exact playbook auditors use, what they're looking for, and how to prepare your case for the best possible outcome. He represents clients at all levels including IRS Appeals and Tax Court.
