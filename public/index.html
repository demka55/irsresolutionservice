How it works

From notice to resolved

You pay once. Romeo and his team take it from there — reviewing your records, building your plan, and filing everything needed to resolve your situation.

01

You authorize access to your IRS records

After payment, you'll receive instructions to grant our team access to your IRS records. Romeo sends a formal request through the IRS website — you simply approve it. No paperwork, no forms to fill out.

02

Romeo reviews your situation — 3 to 5 days

Our team pulls your official transcripts, cross-references every figure in the notice against your actual IRS account, and identifies any errors. Romeo then builds a customized resolution plan specific to your situation.

03

We share the plan — you approve it

We walk you through exactly what we found and what we recommend. No surprises. Most situations are fully resolved within the $999 fee — if your case is complex, we'll tell you upfront before any additional work begins.

04

We file everything and resolve it

Once you approve the plan, our team prepares and files all necessary documents with the IRS. You don't have to write a single letter or make a single call. And for any future notices, you get full access to our free IRS notice decoder.
